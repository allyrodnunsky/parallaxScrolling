As Maya left the party that her friend's forced her to attend she never thought she'd be thanking them for bringing her. The only thing she could get out of her head was the dreamy boy who caught her eye. He smiled at her and she would never forget the sparkle in his pearly whites.

She dreamt about meeting this man again. Much to her surprise she recieved a knock at the door the following day. It was him. He said his name was Lance.

"Hi Maya! I apologize if I'm interupting, but would you want to go out with me. I just can't stop thinking about you"

This was to Maya's surprise. How did he find her house? How did he even know her name? But Maya agreed and they immediately fell head over heels. They spent days together and life was like a fantasy.

Their chemistry was clear from the start and Maya couldn't resist Lance. She waited all day to see him and talk to him. Lance seemed intruiged, but something was holding him back. Maya didn't understand how he couldn't feel the same for her.

One day Lance said to Maya, "I just don't think I have the time to have a girlfriend right now." Maya was crushed.

She couldn't understand what she had done. She tried to ask Lance, but he was hesitant to tell the truth. She loved him and she knew it, but her heart was already broken. She tried to keep herself at a distance but it seemed impossible.

She cried day and night about Lance. She couldn't undertand what he had done. But one day, everything changed.

She's not sure what happened, but Lance called her and asked to go on a date. Sparks flew almost immediately. It was clear Lance was struggling to pretend he didn't want Maya too.

"I love you" Lance stated. His cheeks turned bright pink thinking maybe he said it too soon. But Maya responded and said "I love you too".

They continued to be together forever. Nothing could come between them and it was clear it was love at first sight.
